Values

Privacy is the soil.
Everything grows from it.

Most software is built around your attention. Ours is built around your life. We believe the tools you use every day shouldn't harvest your data, track your behavior, or sell your patterns to the highest bidder.

These aren't product features. They're convictions that were here before the first line of code.

No ads. Ever.

We don't run ads and we never will. Our incentives align with yours.

No trackers.

We don't follow you around the web. What's yours stays yours.

No surveillance.

Your data is for you. Not for training. Not for sale. Not negotiable.
